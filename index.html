White Bread

Ingredients

  • 11 cups of flour
  • 2 t. of yeast
  • 6 t. sugar
  • 6 t. oil
  • 4 t. salt
  • 1 c. milk
  • 3 c. water
  • Equipment:kettle, large mixing bowl, large mixing spoon(wooden), 4 cup glass measuring cup, plastic wrap, dish towel,baking sheet

Steps

  1. Put water in a kettle and turn on to heat up.
  2. Put 4 cups of flour and all dry Ingredients into a very large bowl and stir to combine.
  3. Pour oil and milk into large glass measuring cup. If you dont have a large measuring cup, combine Ingredients
  4. start adding the hot water. When you have about a half cup of water left feel water and keep stirring and feeling the mixxed liquids as you add the water. If its to hot, add a little cold water . If its too cold, add more of the hot water it should be hot enough that you should feel a reaction to sticking in your finger, but not not enough that you want to pull your finger out
  5. mix the liquids into the flour with a large spoon
  6. pour remaining flour into the bowl, stirring as you go along. When its too hard to stir, add flour onto your spoon and scrap it clean. Start kneading
  7. stop adding flour or add more flour to get a consistency where its no longer sticking to your fingers
  8. Knead about ten minutes. You cant overknead when you are doing it by hand. You lift the bread onto its end, press down, turn the bowl, and repeat.
  9. Stop when its satiny and it springs back right awat when you press on it.
  10. Pour oil around the edge of the bowl. Spin the dough around inthe oil to cover the dough and the bowl with oil.
  11. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and then a towel.
  13. if you are in a hurry, put it in a warm place.
  14. After it has doubled in size, punch it down and form it how youd like to cook ot loaf, bread sticks,rolls,etc.
  15. Let it rise another 40 minutes before baking at 400 degree in the oven for 20 minutes.
  16. Take it out an dget it out of the pan onto a cooling rack. Finished bread sounds hollow when you knock on the bottom.

    Red Lentil Soup

    Ingredients

    • 2 carrots
    • 1 medium onion
    • 1 1/2 c. red lentils
    • 1 t. vegeta
    • salt and pepper as desired
    • Equipment: soup pot, stirring spoon, sharp knife, grater

    Steps

    1. Grate two carrots.
    2. Finely dice a medium onion.
    3. Saute the onion in a splotch of oil in the pot. Traditionally youd add a tablespoon or 2 of tomato paste
    4. Add 7 cups of water, the carrots, and lentils.
    5. Season with vegeta.
    6. Bring to a boil and then turn to the lowwest simmer
    7. It will take about an hour for the lentils to cook

        8. Notes: You can let it cook for as long as you like, but keep it on very low heat and stir whenever you walk past
